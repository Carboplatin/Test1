Hello! My name is Alex, and I want to help you build a better version of yourself!

Sport has always played a big part in my life, as a child I joined a local gymnastic team to compete in trampolining. Afterwards I progressed into team sports- amateur football league before I eventually fell in love with volleyball where I played for a number of years before a career ending injury.

The rehabilitation process got me interested in the theory and practical application of strength and conditioning training. My interest shifted from ‘’ how much ‘’ to ‘’how to’’ for exercises. I have looked into a number of programming and fundamentals in a variety of training methods, from powerlifting to pole dancing. Gathering experiences and knowledge became my passion.

Personal Training

My goal as your Personal Trainer is to familiarize, and make sure you are comfortable with useful exercises. Some easy, some very tasking but rewarding, moving you to your final goal! I believe that understanding the body mechanics will make you more confident and what is more important will help you keep the correct form whilst exercising. My ultimate goal is to share my knowledge with you and give you the correct tools to achieve your dreams.

I am a very kind person with a friendly smile but trust me as your personal trainer I will push you to your limits.
